why it exists

One Bottle, One Home, No Scattered Tabs

The hub keeps the studio from turning into forty open tabs, a Discord nobody checks, and a folder called projects_REAL_v3. If something exists, it's findable. If something's planned, it says so — no vague "coming soon" forever.
