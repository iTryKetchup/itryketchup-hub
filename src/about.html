About iTryKetchup Studio

A one-bottle studio for small games, audio stories, web experiments, and honest patch notes - served from the test kitchen without pretending the whole place is a giant company.

  • Build: small, practical, playable or public things.
  • Ship: real pages and working releases before big promises.
  • Log: the useful decisions, not just the shiny screenshots.
