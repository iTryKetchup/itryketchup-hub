## 3. Game Snapshot

Kitchen Wars: The Door is live as a verified standalone MVP. It is a small browser Canvas game where players switch lanes, shoot incoming enemies, jump low obstacles, duck high obstacles, and try to survive while building a local score.

The current playable version includes:

- 3-lane movement
- shooting
- jumping and ducking
- enemies and obstacles
- score tracking
- restart flow
- local browser leaderboard
